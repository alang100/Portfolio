About Me

I am a Data Analyst with a strong foundation in data wrangling, transformation, and visualization, currently contributing to the Department of Education and Workplace Relations (DEWR). I specialize in leveraging statistical modelling, machine learning, and exploratory data analysis to uncover insights that drive policy development and strategic decision-making. With a Master of Data Science acquired from the University of Canberra in 2023, and 24+ years of experience in telecom network optimization, data analytics and project management, I bring a strong blend of technical and analytical expertise to solve complex problems. My passion lies in creating impactful dashboards, predictive models, and actionable insights that add value to organizations.

I am always interested in new and exciting opportunities and I am seeking a challenging position that utilizes my skills and experience in data science and data analysis.
